UNIT 3: JWT EXPLOITATION — EASY
=================================

TARGET: PulseBoard - Team Dashboard
OBJECTIVE: Escalate your privileges to access the admin panel.

CREDENTIALS:
  Username: user
  Password: user123

GETTING STARTED:
  1. Visit the application and log in with the credentials above.
  2. After login, the app will display your JWT token on the dashboard.
  3. JWTs have three parts separated by dots: header.payload.signature
  4. You can decode JWT tokens at jwt.io or with base64 decoding.
  5. Think about what claims control your access level.

TOOLS YOU MAY NEED:
  - Browser Developer Tools
  - jwt.io (online JWT decoder/encoder)
  - curl with Authorization header
  - Understanding of JWT structure (header, payload, signature)

NOTE: The admin panel is visible in the navigation. Accessing it with your
      current token will show an error explaining what's required.
