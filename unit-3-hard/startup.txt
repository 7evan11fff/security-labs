UNIT 3: JWT EXPLOITATION — HARD
=================================

TARGET: SentinelOps - Security Operations Center
OBJECTIVE: Escalate your privileges to access classified operational secrets.

CREDENTIALS:
  Username: operator
  Password: sentinel!ops

GETTING STARTED:
  1. Visit the application and log in with the credentials above.
  2. The dashboard shows your session token and current claims.
  3. Explore the platform's configuration endpoint to understand
     what restricted endpoints exist and what they require.
  4. JWT tokens are cryptographically signed. The security of the signature
     depends entirely on the secret used.
  5. The platform has noted their secret has been "flagged as weak."
  6. Multiple claims may need to be modified simultaneously to gain access.

TOOLS YOU MAY NEED:
  - jwt.io or Python's PyJWT library
  - curl with custom headers
  - Understanding of JWT HS256 signing and secret brute-forcing
  - Attention to error messages (they tell you exactly what's wrong)

NOTE: This challenge requires modifying multiple JWT claims and discovering
      the signing secret. Error messages are your guide.
